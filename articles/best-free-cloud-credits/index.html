Best Free Cloud Credits for Developers 2026

Cloud infrastructure is the biggest expense for most startups. Here are the best free cloud credit programs that can save you thousands on AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, and more.

Top Cloud Credit Programs for Startups

AWS Activate offers $1,000-$100,000 in credits for qualifying startups. Google Cloud for Startups provides $2,000-$350,000 depending on tier. Microsoft for Startups gives $1,000-$150,000 in Azure credits. Cloudflare for Startups offers up to $250,000 in enterprise credits. DigitalOcean Startups provides up to $100,000 over 12 months.

Free Cloud Credits Without a Startup

Not a startup? You can still get free cloud credits. AWS gives $200 free to every new user ($100 on signup + $100 for completing onboarding tasks). Google Cloud has a $300 free trial for 90 days. DigitalOcean offers $200 for 60 days. Oracle Cloud provides 4 ARM cores + 24GB RAM free forever. Vultr gives $100 free credit.

Hosting Platforms for Developers

Netlify offers 300 free credits/month with unlimited deploy previews and serverless functions. Vercel provides $10/month free hosting for Next.js apps. Cloudflare Pages gives unlimited static sites with 500 monthly builds. Fly.io offers $10/month free credits for Docker-based apps. Render includes $5/month free tier for web services.
